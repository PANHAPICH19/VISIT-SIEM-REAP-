Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat largest and most famous temple complex. Built in the 12th century, it began as a Hindu temple and later became a Buddhist site. Known for its grand architecture, intricate carvings, and five-tower design, it’s a UNESCO World Heritage site and popular for sunrise views.

Ta Prohm Temple

Ta Prohm Temple is famous for its unique fusion of ancient ruins and nature. Built in the late 12th century, the temple is enveloped by massive tree roots, creating a mystical, overgrown look.

Ktom Sre Khmer Food

No 10, Ph Srah Srong Tboung Srah Srang South, Siem Reap

Open Hours: Mon-Sun 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Ktom Sre Khmer is deeply rooted in the rural lifestyle of Cambodia, where people rely on locally sourced ingredients. The dish reflects the ingenuity of Cambodian villagers who make the most of what is available, turning simple ingredients into something delicious and nourishing.

WILD - Creative Bar & Spring Rolls Restaurant

Wat Damnak, Aleah Road, Krong Siem Reap 17000, Cambodia
Open Hours: Mon-Sun 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
Wild is a fingerfood restaurant and a Cocktail Bar known for revisiting an asian culinary monument: the world famous Spring Rolls, in more than 15 ways. You will discover fresh and fried rolls like no where else, from the great classics to the most original recipes, in a traditional wooden house and its lush garden, with a friendly and easy going atmosphere.

សច្ចៈ - Satcha Café & Restaurant

Chanrey Tree

Mondul 1 Village, Svay Dangkum Commune, Pokambor Ave, Krong Siem Reap 17252

Open Hours: Mon-Sun 11AM-2:30PM, 5:30PM-10:30PM
Chanrey Tree is a popular riverside restaurant in Siem Reap offering authentic Khmer cuisine in a stylish, traditional setting. Known for dishes like amok trey (steamed fish curry) and lok lak (stir-fried beef), it blends classic Cambodian flavors with modern presentation. Set in a charming wooden house, the restaurant provides a refined dining experience, perfect for those looking to enjoy high-quality Cambodian food with a touch of elegance.

Butterfly Pea Restaurant
